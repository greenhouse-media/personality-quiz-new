Painted turtle

Chrysemys picta

Terrific! You’re Chrysemys picta, the painted turtle. This aquatic reptile shares your love of the water and occupies wetlands where it feeds on a wide range of organisms including aquatic plants, algae, worms, insects, and frogs. You’re prone to getting chilled easily, and this turtle is no different. As an ectotherm, it needs external sources of heat to warm up and can often be found basking on logs and rocks. You may not be especially vocal, but you’re both social animals who like being around others. Large groups of up to 50 turtles have been seen sharing a log and soaking up the warmth of the sun.
