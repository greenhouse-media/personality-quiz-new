Freshwater Mussel

Over 300 Species in North America

Magnificent! You're a freshwater mussel or pearly mussel. Like you, this mussel is as happy as a clam when it’s in the water. You’re also perfectly content just hanging out. Likewise, juvenile and adult mussels nestle themselves into the sand or gravel of the riverbed where they filter tiny bits of food out of the water. In fact, when lots of mussels are around, their filtering can clean the water for improve the environment for all the other species in the river!
